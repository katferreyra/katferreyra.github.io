About me

Hi! I'm Katherine, but you can call me Kat. I've been an avid reader for as long as I can remember. Though I love language on paper, I also have a deep interest in computer languages. Though I don't have any formal training in coding languages, I love investigating and playing around with code. I've been curious and interested in computer languages since Myspace and Xanga were popular social media outlets. I used to create and edit custom layouts using HTML and CSS.

I also love transforming content into easily digestible chunks. Nothing irks me more than word vomit and complicating an already complex concept or idea. Cut to the chase!

Combining these 2 interests led me to my career as a technical writer in the semiconductor industry at Marvell where I create and edit concise, accessible, and accurate documentation.
